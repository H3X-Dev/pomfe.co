FAQ


What is Pomfe.co?

Pomfe.co is a simple file hosting website. It lets you share your photos, documents, videos, or any other type of file privately.

What content is allowed to be uploaded?

It's fine to upload any type of content as long it's not against the laws of EU or Lithuania and you have the legal right to make content available in our service.

Do you keep any logs of uploaded content or users?

We don't log anything (Error Logs, Access Logs, User Logs).

Copyright/Legal Stuff

Pomfe.co respects takedowns for copyrighted content or illegal content. Send a e-mail to votton@nyanpasu.tv (DMCA preferred or Court Order)
